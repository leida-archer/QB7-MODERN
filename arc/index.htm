Image size:
Even though there is a 6 megabyte maximum on image uploads, most web images dont need to be over 100 kilobytes. Many are much smaller and still retain acceptable clarity.

Uploading images of over a meg can diminish the viewers experience as download times get excessive.
Resizing and cropping software is free and readily available. There are also many, easy to use, image editing websites.

Image names:
There are characters on your keyboard that some upload programs will see, not as a part of the image name, but rather as an instruction which it will try to carry out, sometimes disrupting the file transfer. Things like quotation marks, semi colons, exclamation points, even spaces, are problematic.To be safe, stay with alpha-numerics and the underscore... and it would be good if you kept the name under 30 characters.
